Terms of Service
Last updated March 2026 · placeholder copy for the prototype
1. Using Assemble
Assemble helps you organise your wardrobe and suggests outfit combinations from items you add. You are responsible for the items and details you enter.

2. Accounts
Keep your login details secure. You must be 16 or older to use Assemble.

3. Purchases & credits
Credits and subscriptions are billed as shown at checkout. Shopping links may lead to partner stores; purchases there are governed by that store's terms.

4. Your content
Photos you upload stay private to your account and are processed by AI only to detect and match clothing, with your consent.

We may update these terms and will notify you of material changes in the app.

Privacy Policy
Last updated March 2026 · placeholder copy for the prototype
What we collect
Your account details, the wardrobe items and photos you add, your style preferences, and how you use the app.

How we use it
To detect and match clothing, generate outfit suggestions, and improve the service. Photos are analysed by AI only with your consent.

Sharing
We do not sell your data. Outfits are only shared with friends you choose. Partner stores receive an anonymous referral when you tap a shopping link.

Your controls
You can edit or delete items, remove avatar data, or delete your account at any time in Settings.
